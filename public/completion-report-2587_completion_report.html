Summary

Vegetation management and access reinstatement works were successfully completed between Donnybrook and Wandong. The project involved tree trimming near low voltage conductors, mulching of blackberry and gorse, and clearing under powerlines to ensure safe access for maintenance equipment and personnel. The multi-tiered works spanned over 7 working days and included manual hand cutting, ELC tree trimming at Donnybrook Rd Crossing, and mulching with Kubota and JCB equipment. All gorse, blackberry, and English broom were mulched to ground level, and trees were trimmed for ELC maintenance. The project commenced on 19th May 2025, faced delays due to machinery damage, and was completed on 23rd June 2025.

Key Findings

  • Vegetation was cleared from poles, powerlines, and easements to restore access.
  • Tree trimming was performed to maintain safe distances from low voltage conductors.
  • Mulching of gorse, blackberry, and English broom was completed under powerlines.
  • Multiple specialised machines were used, including excavators and forestry mulchers.
  • Delays occurred due to machinery damage, but all works were completed by 23 June 2025.
