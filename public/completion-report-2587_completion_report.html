V/Line – Completion Report

Summary

The project aimed to complete access track maintenance between Donnybrook and Wandong, focusing on vegetation clearance, tree trimming near low voltage conductors, and mulching under powerlines. The project was successfully completed despite delays due to machinery damage.

Findings & Recommendations

  • Vegetation was cleared from poles, powerlines, and easements.
  • Tree trimming was performed to maintain safe distances from low voltage conductors.
  • Mulching of gorse, blackberry, and English broom was completed outside significant vegetation zones.
  • Multiple machines, including excavators and forestry mulchers, were utilised.
  • Works were delayed due to machinery damage but were completed after repairs.
