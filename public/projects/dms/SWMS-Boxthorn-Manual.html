SWMS — Manual Boxthorn Removal

Donald Mineral Sands — PPA Control Programme

Client: Donald Project Pty Ltd — Steve Talbot
Site: Area 21 (HZ21) and Barry's Block — Minyip, VIC

Scope

This SWMS covers manual removal methods for African Boxthorn (Lycium ferocissimum) including cut-stump herbicide application, hand-pulling, and chipping for material removal. Work specifically excludes any activity that impacts native trees or protected habitat within Area 21 no-go zones.

Responsibilities

  • Supervisor: Ensure crew training, confirm safe systems, monitor environmental controls and Fulcrum records.
  • Operator: Follow method, wear PPE, complete Pre-Start JSB and record activity in Fulcrum.
  • Environmental Officer: Confirm native vegetation buffers and monitor off-target risk.

Hazards & Controls

HazardControl
Sharp branches / cutsCut-resistant gloves, eye protection, long sleeves. First aid kit on site.
Herbicide contact (cut-stump)Use Grazon Extra per label; operator AQF L3; wear chemical-resistant gloves; avoid spray drift; apply directly to stump only
Trip / manual handlingUse correct lifting technique, team lift heavy items, rest rotations.
Off-target native plant damageMark native plants before removal. Use hand methods away from native species. Supervisor sign-off required for any material left on site.

Method (Step-by-step)

  1. Pre-start: Complete Pre-Start Job Safety Briefing — confirm PPE, verify weed species and locations, confirm no-go zones and buffer flags.
  2. Inspect: Check tools (pruners, loppers, machete, handsaw). Ensure chippers are serviceable if used off-site.
  3. Cut-stump: For mature Boxthorn, cut stem near ground. Immediately apply Grazon Extra to the cambial surface using a brush/applicator (per label). Record product, dose, operator in Fulcrum Chemical Use Record.
  4. Hand-pull: For seedlings, pull roots out; bag and remove from offset site. Do not compost on-site.
  5. Chipping: If chipping allowed outside offset area, use chipper on a separate vehicle and remove chips off-site. Do not place chips near native vegetation.
  6. Clean down: After work, clean tools and boots at hygiene point. Submit TMC Vehicle Hygiene Record before leaving site.

Emergency & Spill

Spill kit must be present. For chemical splashes to skin — flush 15+ minutes, seek medical advice, call Poisons Info 13 11 26. For large spills, contain, block drains and notify supervisor and EPA if waterways threatened.

Records

  • Pre-Start Job Safety Briefing — Fulcrum
  • Chemical Use Record — Fulcrum (cut-stump application entries)
  • TMC Enviro Works Ver 2 — treatment and photo evidence
  • TMC Vehicle Hygiene Record — site entry/exit
Stop Rules:
  1. Do not commence if AQF L3 operator not present for chemical tasks.
  2. Do not applicate Grazon Extra if native vegetation risk within buffer zone is not cleared.
  3. Stop if wind conditions risk drift.
