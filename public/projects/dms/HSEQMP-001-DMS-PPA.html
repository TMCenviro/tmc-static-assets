1. Purpose

Provide crews and supervisors with the HSEQ controls and procedures required to deliver pest plant and animal (PPA) control works across the Donald Mineral Sands project area, including manual boxthorn removal, herbicide works and rabbit warren control. This HSEQMP supports compliance with EPBC 2005/2372 and Landowner Agreement CW-CFL-3840-01.

2. Scope

Applies to TMC enviro staff and approved subcontractors delivering manual and chemical PPA works on DMS land, DMS-controlled road reserves, Area 21 (HZ21) and Barry's Block. Activities include manual removal (cut stump, hand pull, chipping), herbicide application (hose reel, hooded spot spray), and rabbit fumigation (Phostoxin, Schedule 7).

5. Control Measures — Manual Boxthorn Removal

  • Wear gloves, eye protection, long sleeves, gaiters and puncture-resistant boots.
  • For mature Boxthorn: cut-stump method — cut stem close to ground and apply Grazon Extra to stump immediately (within 30 seconds).
  • Hand-pull seedlings where possible; treat root collars and bag removed plants for off-site disposal (do not compost).
  • Do not disturb native plants; use brush-cutting only where no native species will be impacted.
  • Collect and remove all plant material from offset area unless DMS directs otherwise. No burning on site without permit.

6. Control Measures — Herbicide Application

Operators must:

  • Be AQF Level 3 qualified for chemical application.
  • Wear PPE per SDS (gloves, goggles, chemical-resistant coveralls, respirator when required).
  • Check weather conditions — do not spray if wind >3 m/s or rain is imminent.
  • Use hooded applicator or hose reel inside offset block; boom spray only where prior inspection confirms no native plant risk.
  • Record every application in Fulcrum Chemical Use Record (product, rate, operator, GPS, photos).

7. Control Measures — Rabbit Fumigation

Only Schedule 7 authorised operators may apply Phostoxin. Follow NATSOP RAB004:

  1. Map all warrens and record active vs inactive in Fulcrum (GPS).
  2. Brief crew and set exclusion zone (48–72 hours). Sign and barricade area.
  3. Apply Phostoxin tablets to warrens as per NATSOP instructions; seal entrances.
  4. Return at specified time to remove carcasses — collect and remove off-site (prevent secondary poisoning).
  5. Log tablet count, operator name, and carcass disposal records in Fulcrum.

9. Records & Evidence

All field evidence must be captured in Fulcrum on the same day. Required forms: Pre-Start Job Safety Briefing, Chemical Use Record, TMC Vehicle Hygiene Record, TMC Enviro Works Ver 2 (treatment and warren mapping), IMS Incident & Investigation Report, TMC Field Audit Form.

10. Stop Rules

  1. Do not commence if AQF L3 operator is not present for herbicide works.
  2. Do not fumigate if Schedule 7 authorisation cannot be verified for the operator.
  3. Do not spray if wind >3 m/s or rain forecast within 4 hours.
  4. Do not proceed if SDS is not available on site.
  5. Do not proceed if vehicle hygiene record not completed before offset site entry.
