TMC-SWI-203 — Chemical Weed Spraying Procedures (SAPR-80)

Domain: WHS & Environmental — v2.0
Owner: Operations Manager  |  Related: TMC-CP-002.2, TMC-SWMS-DMS-001

Purpose

Field-level instruction for safe mixing, loading and application of herbicides for weed control on the DMS PPA programme. Aligns to SAPR-80 and TMC CP procedures.

Operational Steps

  1. Verify chemical: Confirm APVMA registration and label before use.
  2. Inspect equipment: Pumps, hoses, nozzles, pressure and hood/boom checks.
  3. Weather check: No spraying if wind > 3 m/s or rain within 4 hours.
  4. Prepare spill kit: Accessible, checked, team briefed on containment.
  5. Apply with caution: Hooded/hose-reel preferred in offsets; continuous drift monitoring.
  6. Record: Chemical Use Record in Fulcrum (product, rate, GPS, photos).

PPE & Controls

ChemicalScheduleMinimum PPE
Weedmaster 450S5Gloves, safety glasses, long sleeve shirts
Grazon ExtraS6Chemical-resistant gloves, goggles, face shield, coveralls, boots
Oxyfluorfen 240 ECS6Gloves, goggles, coveralls — H360 caution

Don’t Proceed If

  1. Equipment damaged or leaking
  2. Chemical unverified against label
  3. Weather unsuitable
  4. Spill kit absent

Record Keeping

Use Fulcrum: Pre-Start Job Safety Briefing, Chemical Use Record, TMC Vehicle Hygiene Record, IMS Incident & Investigation Report if any incident occurs.

Emergency

For chemical exposure, follow SDS first aid and call Poisons Information 13 11 26. For major incidents call 000.
