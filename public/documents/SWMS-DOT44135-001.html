L4×C3

  • Team lifts for rolls over 20 kg — no single-person lift of full roll

  • Pre-Start JSB not completed in Fulcrum for today’s shift  |  MOA-0091800 not yet activated via RAPP or TOC (1300 107 757) — no TCDs to be deployed before activation
  • TGS TA052611 A/B/C not fully set up OR TMA not deployed  |  Certified Traffic Controller not on site and confirmed for this shift
  • Site conditions materially different from those described in this SWMS — re-brief all crew before proceeding
