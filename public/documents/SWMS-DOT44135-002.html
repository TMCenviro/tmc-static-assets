6 Coir Matting Installation on Slopes
Roll out, peg and secure erosion blanket per DSE26032REP01		 Manual handling on steep wet slopes, slip/fall on embankment, snake/fauna encounter, matting spring-back HIGH
L4×C3
=12
  • 3-point contact on embankment at all times — no running or rushing on grades
  • Minimum 2 persons for all matting on slopes steeper than 1:3 — no solo slope work
  • Probe vegetation before putting hands to ground — snake/fauna awareness on embankment
  • Waterproof non-slip footwear mandatory for wet embankment work
  • Install top-of-bank to bottom — never unroll from below
  • Peg spacing per DSE26032REP01 (max 1 m) — ensure full contact with ground, no bridging
  • Completed installation inspected by Supervisor — photo evidence in Fulcrum before next section

10 Plant Recovery Operations
If excavator or SVL97-3 becomes bogged or stuck on soft embankment adjacent to live freeway		 Plant sliding toward live carriageway, crushing of recovery personnel, secondary bogging of recovery vehicle, snap-back of recovery lines, overloading of anchor points, live traffic exposure during extended recovery operation EXT
L3×C5
=15
  • STOP IMMEDIATELY if plant begins to bog — do not attempt to drive plant further. Operator to leave plant in gear/park with boom lowered if safe to do so
  • Account for all personnel before any recovery action — no one within 2× recovery line length of any tensioned line during recovery
  • Notify Supervisor immediately — Supervisor assesses situation before any recovery attempt
  • Do NOT attempt self-recovery with another bogged or undersized vehicle — use professional heavy haulage recovery only
  • Heavy recovery vehicle contact confirmed at pre-start JSB — operator has number on person
  • TM must remain fully operational throughout recovery — notify TC and consider extending TM footprint if recovery vehicle required on shoulder
  • Notify DTP/client (Greg Woodburn 0458 922 033) if recovery will cause extended traffic management beyond MOA-0091800 parameters
  • Recovery lines/slings rated for plant weight (5.5t excavator + SVL97-3 up to 5.4t) — check before attachment
  • All personnel behind cover (vehicle body) during tensioning of recovery lines — never stand in line of potential snap-back
  • Post-recovery: inspect plant for damage before returning to service — record in Fulcrum Equipment Hub
  • Incident report raised in Fulcrum regardless of outcome — bogging events recorded
