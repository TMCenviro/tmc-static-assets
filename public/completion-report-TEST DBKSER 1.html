V/Line – Completion Report

Summary

TMC Enviro successfully completed a vegetation clearing project for V/Line along the corridor from 55.500 (Epping-Kilmore Rd crossing) to 58.630 (Hume Freeway) in Wandong, Victoria. The project involved the removal of woody weeds and the creation of access for ARTC pole line maintenance. The work was executed using a 5t Excavator with a Seppi Mulcher and a Skid Steer with a Seppi Forestry Mulcher over a period of five full days. Approximately 60% of the cleared area can be maintained with conventional equipment, while the remainder requires specialized machinery due to limited access. The project addressed the hazard of ARTC signal cables being below the minimum height, ensuring safety and compliance.
