Summary

TMC Enviro installed two heavy-duty steel gates at the entry and exit of the reopened access road between Walkers Road and Walls Road, Macedon. The gates meet V/Line’s requirements for emergency vehicle access. Underground cable protection was ensured through non-destructive digging, following identification of Optus and VicTrack cables. The installation phase was completed over two days following several weeks of planning and stakeholder coordination to ensure safe and compliant delivery.

Key Findings

  • Two heavy-duty steel gates installed at both ends of the access road.
  • Gates meet V/Line’s minimum 3.5 metre opening width for emergency vehicles.
  • Provision for future bollards included; V/Line to supply locks.
  • Optus and VicTrack underground cables identified and protected.
  • Non-destructive digging used for all post hole excavations.
