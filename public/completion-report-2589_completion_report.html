Access Track Maintenance Donnybrook - Wandong Completion Report

Client: V/Line

Location: Wandong, Victoria

Date Completed: 2025-06-30

Summary

The project aimed to improve access and reduce fire hazards by completing vegetation mulching and removal works along the access track between Epping-Kilmore Road and Wandong Station. The outcome was successful, with all blackberry and gorse cleared, particularly under and around powerlines, effectively mitigating fire hazards behind the Wandong Shopping Centre and within the easement.

Key Findings

  • All blackberry and gorse vegetation was mulched and removed along the specified corridor.
  • Vegetation clearance was completed under and around powerlines to improve access.
  • Fire hazards behind the Wandong Shopping Centre were effectively mitigated.
  • Access for ARTC to power poles was created as per scope.
  • Works extended from the rail street crossing to Wandong Station.
