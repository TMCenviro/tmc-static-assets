Purpose & Use

This master hazard library provides reusable hazard modules for SWMS and HSEQMP drafting. Agents must only select modules relevant to the job and customise controls to site conditions, client requirements, permits and plant.

Risk matrix (IMS-aligned)

Likelihood (1–5) × Consequence (1–5) = Risk
Low 1–4 | Medium 5–9 | High 10–14 | Extreme 15–25. R ≥ 10 = significant.
