Summary

TMC Enviro successfully completed the installation of two heavy-duty steel gates at the entry and exit points of the reopened access road between Walkers Road and Walls Road in Macedon, Victoria. The project, which was completed on 11 July 2025, adhered to V/Line specifications for emergency vehicle access and included essential underground cable protection measures. Notably, the gates were designed with a minimum opening width of 3.5 metres to facilitate emergency vehicle access, including fire trucks. The installation was executed following meticulous planning and stakeholder coordination, employing non-destructive digging methods to safeguard existing underground assets.

Key Findings

  • Two heavy-duty steel gates were installed at both ends of the access road between Walkers Road and Walls Road.
  • Gates meet V/Line's minimum 3.5 metre opening width for emergency vehicle access.
  • Non-destructive digging methods were used to protect Optus Optical Fibre and VicTrack underground cables.
  • Provision for future bollards was included in the installation.
  • V/Line will supply the locks for the gates as arranged.
