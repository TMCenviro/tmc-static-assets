Summary

TMC Enviro installed two heavy-duty steel gates at the entry and exit points of the reopened access road between Walkers Road and Walls Road. The gates meet V/Line’s emergency access requirements, and underground cable protection measures were implemented to safeguard existing assets. Non-destructive digging (NDD) was used for all post hole excavations due to the presence of Optus Optical Fibre and VicTrack underground cables. The installation phase was completed over two days following several weeks of planning and stakeholder coordination to ensure safe and compliant delivery.
