V/Line – Completion Report

Summary

The vegetation clearing project along the railway corridor from 58.6kp to the Hume Freeway was completed by TMC Enviro on behalf of V/Line. The project involved the removal of woody weeds and the creation of access to ARTC pole lines for maintenance purposes. The work was executed using a 5t Excavator with Seppi Mulcher and a Kubota SVL95 Positrack with a Seppi Forestry Mulcher. Approximately 60% of the area was maintained with conventional tractor slasher combinations, while the remaining 40% required specialized equipment. The project was completed over five full days, with documentation including 13 photos and 3 videos highlighting vegetation to signal cable hazards.
