V/Line – Completion Report

Summary

TMC Enviro successfully completed a vegetation clearing project for V/Line along the railway corridor from 58.6kp to the Hume Freeway. The project involved the removal of woody weeds and the provision of access for ARTC maintenance. The work was executed over five days using a 5-ton excavator, Seppi Mulcher, and Kubota SVL95 Positrack. The location of the project was 35 Sir Leo Curtis Drive, Wandong, Victoria, 3758, Australia. The project was completed without any incidents, and approximately 60% of the area is now maintainable with conventional equipment. The clearing also addressed potential hazards related to vegetation and signal cables, as documented in the project videos.
