Summary

This project involved vegetation management and access track reinstatement between 50.600 KP and 55.254 KP along the Donnybrook to Wandong corridor, in accordance with SRF requirements and QU-2583. The project was completed on 2025-07-11 and included heavy mulching of woody weeds and blackberry under overhead powerlines, clearing around poles, and ensuring all travel surfaces were safe and accessible for future inspections. All processed vegetation was mulched and retained onsite, with access created to all relevant infrastructure. Key findings include that vegetation was cleared and mulched to meet requirements, access tracks were reinstated, and travel surfaces were left safe and accessible.

Key Findings

  • Vegetation was cleared and mulched between 50.600 KP and 55.254 KP to meet SRF and QU-2583 requirements.
  • Access tracks were reinstated and prepared for future ELC inspections.
  • Heavy mulching was performed under overhead powerlines using specialised equipment.
  • All processed vegetation was retained onsite, and bases of poles were cleared for safe access.
  • Travel surfaces were left safe, accessible, and free of vegetation obstructions.
